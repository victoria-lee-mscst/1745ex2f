Directions

From Rt. 495:

Take exit 29A East on Rt. 2 for 4 miles to exit 42, Rt. 27. Turn left off exit ramp & proceed approximately 2 miles. Entrance to plaza is just before intersection on left.

From Rt. 128:

Take exit 29 onto Rt. 2 West and continue to the Concord rotary. Take the second spoke off the rotary onto Rt. 2A/119. Follow Rt. 2A for 2.7 miles to traffic lights at intersection of 2A and 27.
