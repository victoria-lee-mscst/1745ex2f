Welcome

Welcome to Victoria's Coffee Shop located in Acton, Massachusetts. We specialize in gourmet coffee and delicious pastries in an exciting and artistic atmosphere.

Victoria's is an independent cafe that offers some of the best espresso drinks using premium roasted beans, teas, chai, gourmet coffee, frozen drinks and pastries. Some of our coffee beans include Fair Trade Nicaraguan, Italian, French, and Sumatra.
